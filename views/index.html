Submit your password and we'll check the Have I been pwned? list of exposed passwords (using the Have I been pwned? Pwned Passwords API) to see if yours is in there. If it is, we'll tell you how many times it appears.

Notes

We use a range search to check for your password's exposure, and all of the hashing and comparing is done in your browser. Only the first 5 characters of the hash is sent to the Have I been pwned? site so your anonymity is maintained.

We rely on the Web Crypto API's SubtleCrypto methods, which only "modern" browsers support. Basically, this site might not work in IE, Opera, or Safari.
